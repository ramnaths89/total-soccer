How many players per side?

This affects the pitch feel, AI complexity, and how close to the original Total Soccer it plays

A

11 vs 11 — Full Match

Authentic football. Full pitch, all positions (GK, DEF, MID, FWD). Faithful to Total Soccer (1997). More complex AI but true to the game.

B

7 vs 7 — Arcade Mode

Faster, more action. Easier to implement. Good for quick matches. Loses some of the tactical feel of the original.

C

5 vs 5 — Street / Mini Soccer

Very arcade. Fast and chaotic. Fun but drifts far from Total Soccer's original feel.
